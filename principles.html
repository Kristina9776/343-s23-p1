Psych Ninja Principles

How to Master the Secret Art of Learning in Stealth

At first difficult, these techniques will become reflexive with practice and, like all ninja techniques, highly efficient.

  1. There is no psych Ninja

    Learning is ultimately a solitary journey.

  2. Be Responsible.

    Each of us in in charge of our own learning. Teachers cannot learn for you.

  3. Be Diligent.

    Learning is work: Do the work yourself or suffer the consequences. Hard work in training will make studying easier when it counts.

  4. Be Efficient.

    Well trained Ninjas do not think, they react. Although effortful at firts, practice, good techniques can become second nature. Get good at it and it will seem like less work (tdlr, become efficient).

  5. Know Yourself.

    While most things work for most people and some things don't work for anyone, not every weapon is useful to every Ninja given their personal style and goals (immediate as well as long-term goals). The goal of a Psych Ninji is to first know yourself and what works for you in given context.

  6. Be Resourceful.

    Experiment with techniques to achieve perfection while committing 100% to none.

  7. Be Flexible.

    Have many weapons and know how and when to use them: Not all techniques work with all material in all contexts for all people. Be flexible, knowledgeable and resourceful.

  8. Be Fluid.

    If what you are doing doesn't work do something else.

  9. Be Forgiving.

    If a technigue does not work, learn from it and move on. Do not dwell on mistakes, learn from them.
