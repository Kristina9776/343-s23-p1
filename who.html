Dr. David Daniel

Who is Dr. Daniel? Your professor. Cryptic and hilarious, David Daniel is an award-winning teacher with over 25 years of classroom experience. Dr. Daniel enjoys working collaboratively with students and other faculty to translate evidence-based strategies and sustainable teaching to learning practice across disciplines. He can help you with studying tips, testing techniques, or answering any questions you may have about psychology. Want to know a little more about his background? Check out his website!
