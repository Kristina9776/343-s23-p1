What Even Happens in There?

Well...a lot of stuff.

Reading techniques

Work with Dr. Daniel or the TA's to make reaading less of a chore. Using Dr. Daniel's method of reading may seem daunting at first, but give it a try, it might surprise you. This method of reading uses active recall to practice pulling the information that you stored in your brain. More work upfront means less work later on when you're studying, because you have already practiced retrieving that information. Want to find out more? Stop by and see us!

Review Notes

Miss something in lecture? Wrote something down but you don't really know what it means? Fear not! Pair up with one of the TA's and we can help you go through and figure everything out. We can also help you be more efficient with your notes and teach you ways that will help you cater your notes towards your understanding.

Make Studying Fun!

Studying by yourself can be boring and you can lose focus. Come into the TA room and study with the TA's and other students! You can utalize the white board to write and draw out concepts and we do a lot of hands on learning as well! Just wait until you get into the chapter about learning with classical and operant conditioning. Does the name pavlov ring any bells?

Testing Can Be Scary...

We get it, no one likes taking tests. However, we can show you a few tips and tricks to make it less stressful. After you take your exam you can always come in to review it with one of the TA's. We can help you work through any of the problems you missed and make sure you get those concepts down. We can also check to see if there are any patterns in your test-taking that we might be able to help you with.
